About Our Barangay

A Thriving Community in the Heart of Caloocan

Barangay 170 lies within the Deparo zone of North Caloocan's 2nd District, contributing to one of the area's largest and most active residential communities.

The barangay features local schools, small businesses, and neighborhood organizations that keep the community engaged and connected. Its position near the Caloocan–Valenzuela boundary gives residents access to services and employment opportunities in both cities.
